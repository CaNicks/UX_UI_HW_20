Problem Statement

A Little Help was created for older adults to receive help doing the tasks that they can no longer accomplish themselves. However, users are put off by the long application form, potentially causing a decrease in volunteers.

Solution

The goal of this project was to rework the homepage and sign-up page for volunteers by breaking up information and adding more to allow the site to be more user friendly and to allow a user to know what A Little Helps mission is

Competitor Analysis

To get a better idea of how other services and companies assists older members of society, we completed a competitor analysis. This biggest discovery was that all the competitors would only come out to assist if certain criteria such as minimum amount of tasks, set time, or pay by task was meet. This caused many of A Little Helps members to lose out due to the high cost and requirements

Initial Reaserch

The team and I starting our research by first contacting the stakeholder to set up a meeting. We wanted to get to know more about A Little Help since this was an outside project. We continued on by conducting a I Like, I Wish, What If test, before and after out initial round of testing. This allowed us to really delve into the site.

User Flow

With the main focus of the redesign being the Application we really wanted to define exactly how the user would get to the application. We wanted it to be something that ce be found in multiple places without requiring more than 3 clicks to start.

Survey and User Testing

After completing the Competitor Analysis and Initial Research we continued to conduct a survey focused around the user experience when it comes to filling out a form. We wanted to get a better idea of the frustrations they have and how we can avoid them.
